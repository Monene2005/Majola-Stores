About Us

Majola Stores is a proudly small privately owned goods and ware trade business serving the community of Mehlareng.We offer a diverse range of everyday essentials including freshly baked bread, meats, dairy, beverages, canned goods, and locally sourced produce. Our commitment is to provide not just products, but nourishment, convenience, and a sense of community.
